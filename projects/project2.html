Solar Power Forecasting & Weather Analytics

A real-world project combining Python, Power BI, and ML models to predict solar power generation and provide actionable insights for plant operations.

üõ† Technical Workflow

  • Data Collection: Combined AC/DC logs, irradiation, temperature, humidity, and wind speed from two plants.
  • Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering: Handled missing values, outliers, calculated efficiency metrics, and resampled time-series to uniform intervals.
  • Exploratory Analysis: Correlation plots, scatter visualizations, seasonal trends, and operational patterns analyzed using Matplotlib & Seaborn.
  • Modeling: Linear Regression & XGBoost for power prediction, train/test split with cross-validation, hyperparameter tuning.
  • Evaluation: Metrics include RMSE, MAE, and seasonal performance comparison; visualized predicted vs actual outputs.
  • Deployment Insights: Real-time dashboards via Power BI; recommendations for maintenance & inverter optimization.
